Anti Bullying Campaign

Website

Our website is dedicated to raising awareness and combating bullying in basketball. We believe that everyone should feel safe and welcome in the sport, regardless of their identity. That's why we've developed three unique campaign ideas that focus on disability, gender, and race discrimination. Through our portfolio case study, we explore each idea in detail and showcase how they can help create a more inclusive and supportive basketball community. Join us in our mission to end bullying in basketball and promote respect and acceptance for all players

CHAT APP

Our chat app is a powerful tool designed to help visitors to our website connect and communicate with one another. Whether you want to share your thoughts on our campaign to end bullying in basketball or just connect with like-minded individuals, our chat app provides a safe and supportive space to do so. Our goal is to foster a sense of community and encourage dialogue between visitors to our website, and our chat app is the perfect platform to make that happen. Join the conversation today and be part of the movement to create a more inclusive and welcoming basketball community.
